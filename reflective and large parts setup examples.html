Large Parts Adjustments

  • Use 9ft seamless paper (wall to floor)
  • Raise camera height or use wider lens
  • Move lights farther back (60"+)
  • May need overhead light for depth
  • Part sits on floor portion of sweep
  • Use cart/dolly for heavy parts

Large Part Examples

  • Front/rear bumpers
  • Hoods and trunk lids
  • Transmissions
  • Engine blocks
  • Exhaust systems
  • Full dashboards
